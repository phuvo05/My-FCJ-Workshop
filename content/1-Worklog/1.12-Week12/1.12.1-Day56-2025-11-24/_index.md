# Nova 2 Family Highlights

- Sonic: speech-to-speech, multilingual, keeps conversation context, controllable speech
- Lite: fast, cost-effective reasoning with long context window
- Omni (preview): multimodal (text, image, video, speech input) with text/image output
- Forge: program to train custom frontier models on Nova infra

## Agents for UI & Workflows

- Nova Act GA: UI agents for browser tasks (forms, search, booking) targeting >90% reliability
